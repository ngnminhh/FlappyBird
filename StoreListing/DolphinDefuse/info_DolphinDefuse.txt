TITLE: Dolphin Defuse

SHORT:

Help the dolphin dodge bombs by moving them up and down. Boom = game over!

LONG:
🎮 Introducing the Dolphin Dash Mayhem!
Think this game is easy? Think again! Meet a tiny pixel dolphin on a wild journey full of sneaky bombs and tight squeezes. Cute? Yes. Easy? Not a chance! 🐬💥

🕹 Simple gameplay… until you actually play it!
Your job? Move those bombs up and down to create gaps big enough for the dolphin to swim through. Sounds easy — until the dolphin starts moving faster than your reflexes can handle!

▶️ How to start?
Just download the game, tap the Play button, and boom — you're in the action. No long tutorials, just instant fun!

🐟 Wait, you control the WHAT?!
That’s right — you don’t control the dolphin. You control the bombs! 🤯 Slide them up and down to clear a path. One wrong move and your dolphin buddy goes boom. Oops
